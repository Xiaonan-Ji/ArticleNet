Introduction--Learn about us!

The purpose of the project is to recommend articles based on their total scores which reflect not only the similarity to the selected articles, but also the degree of relevance to the specified search strategy, such as publicationtype and biomedical related keywords.


2

Our Methods--How the system works!

We develop out systems in five phases: (1) build the text collection by extracting articles (MEDLINE format) from PubMed; This article list will be input by users in future use; (2) Prepare keywords list, PubType list and medical dictionary. The keywords list and PubType list will be defined by users in future use; (3) Preprocess and parse articles from the list, extract and classify feature terms and remove noisy terms; (4) Process to calculate Mesh Similarity, Abstract similarity, PubType score and Keywords score; (5) Recommend articles based on their total scores; (6) Evaluate the performance regarding different topics and scenarios.
